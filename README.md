# Customer Segmentation and Satisfaction Analysis  
## Venkatesa Refineries Company – Vellakovil, Tiruppur District

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on **Customer Segmentation and Satisfaction Analysis** for Venkatesa Refineries Company located in Vellakovil, Tiruppur District. The study aims to understand customer behavior, preferences, demographics, and satisfaction levels using data visualization techniques in Power BI.

The analysis highlights the company’s strong customer-centric approach through:
- Effective customer segmentation
- High product quality
- Competitive pricing strategies
- Excellent customer service
- Sustainable and innovative business practices

The project demonstrates how data-driven insights can help improve customer relationships, enhance loyalty, and support long-term business growth.

---

# 🎯 Objectives
- To analyze customer demographics and buying behavior
- To identify different customer segments
- To evaluate customer satisfaction levels
- To understand factors influencing customer loyalty
- To improve business strategies through data insights

# 📊 Power BI Visualizations Used

| Visualization Tool | Purpose |
|--------------------|---------|
